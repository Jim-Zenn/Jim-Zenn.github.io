eigenvectors, eigenvalues

positive definite $\iff$ Q > 0

positive semidefinite $\iff$ Q >= 0

for $Q = Q^T \in \R^{n \times n}$, the eigenvalues of $Q $ are real and $Q $ is positive definitive if and only if the eigenvalues $Q $ are positive.

same for semidefinite positive ~ nonnegative.
